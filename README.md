# 👋 Hi, I’m Greg

I’m a product + engineering leader who’s spent most of my career helping technical companies build products people love using - and continue to rely on.  

I’ve spent much of my career in aerospace and embedded systems, leading teams, building products, and helping organizations grow into more durable, long-term businesses.

Recently, I’ve been getting back closer to the tools — rebuilding my hands-on skills in software, embedded systems, and hardware design.

---

## 🧭 What I Do

By day (and for most of my career), I’ve worked across:

- Engineering leadership  
- Product strategy  
- Embedded & electromechanical systems  
- Customer-facing technical products  
- Taking organizations from “it works because of a few people” → “it works because the system works”

I’ve had the opportunity to help multiple companies grow, with a strong belief in doing it at a sustainable pace — more of a “20-mile march” than a sprint.

Most of that work happens in the messy middle:

Spending time close to the product, understanding the details, working through problems with the team, and helping people grow into bigger roles over time.

I think strong organizations are built when leaders stay connected to the work, create clarity where things are messy, and develop people who can eventually do the job better than they could.
